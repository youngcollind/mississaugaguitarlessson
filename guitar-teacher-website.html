Learn Guitar From Home!

Experience the joys of music for yourself through private comprehensive online guitar lessons! I'm Collin Young, I have been teaching guitar for almost two decades and I will happily share with you the many secrets for developing your own musical gifts!

Welcoming students of all ages and backgrounds, I will tailor virtual lessons to suit your musical interests and will work with you at the pace you're most comfortable with.

Through a variety of video conferencing platforms (Skype/Zoom/Messenger) I am able to offer online guitar lessons all around the world. Make an appointment today and see just how exciting learning guitar can be!

Why You Need a Teacher

Learning music from instructional videos can be frustrating and confusing. Some lessons move too slow, some move too fast. By working with you in real time, I am able to answer questions and explain things at exactly your speed. I am able to chart your progress and move you forward with structure, teaching you the right techniques at the right times. You will stay challenged, without feeling overwhelmed!

How It Works

You can conveniently login to your online guitar lessons via your computer, tablet or phone. We'll set up a weekly appointment time that fits into your busy schedule. Virtual lessons are available for 1/2 hour and full hour time frames.

Lesson material is very flexible depending your interests. We can focus on theory, or just have fun playing the songs you enjoy! Students of any age or any skill level are welcome!

You can try out the first online guitar lesson for free to see if it's a good fit for you!

About Collin

I grew up in Mississauga. I first picked up a guitar in 1996 and haven't put it down since. While I was originally self taught, I have since studied music through The National Guitar Workshop and earned a college diploma from Metal Works Institute of Sound and Music Production. I've learned a lot from playing music in bands, having played gigs across Canada. I also work as a recording engineer, and have produced albums for a diverse range of artists. I began teaching guitar lessons in 2003 through the Mississauga Academy of Music and later began my own private tutoring business, most recently making the switch to online guitar lessons.

Bass Lessons

As well providing virtual lessons for acoustic guitar and electric guitar students, I also offer classes specific to the bass guitar. I can show you all the proper tips and techniques to get you started learning the bass today!

Loog Guitars

For younger students who don't feel ready for a full size guitar, Loog guitars can be an excellent start to the instrument. With a Loog, you can start to learn the concepts of guitar, but with only three strings instead of the regular six. I'm able to get kids started with online classes for both Loog and standard guitars, feel free to get in touch to discuss further which might be best for you!
